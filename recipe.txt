Ingredients:
1kg Flour
1kg Cheese
2 Onions
4 Eggs

Instructions:
1. Chop the oninos
2. Heat some water
3. Put the eggs into it
4. Take the water pot from the stove
5. Put the flour into it and mix it. 
6. Make some rounded balls
7. Put some chess and oninons in the middle
8. Fry it
9. Time for a last dance.
